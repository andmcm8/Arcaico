Project Narrative

An aerodynamic glass form that redefines the Oslo skyline, merging sustainable innovation with vertical elegance.

Rising from the icy fjords, Vertex Tower stands as a testament to modern engineering and aesthetic purity. Its elliptical shape is not merely stylistic; the curvature is designed to reduce wind loads significantly while maximizing solar gain during the long Nordic winters.

The double-skin glass façade acts as a thermal buffer, regulating the internal climate and reducing energy consumption by 40%. Inside, the column-free floor plates offer unparalleled flexibility for the workspace of tomorrow, fostering collaboration through uninterrupted sightlines and abundant natural light.

Design Philosophy
"In dense urban environments, architecture must breathe. We sculpt voids as carefully as we build mass, creating moments of pause in the vertical city."
