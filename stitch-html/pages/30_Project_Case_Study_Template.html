Project Narrative

A seamless integration of indoor and outdoor living, this pavilion challenges the boundaries of traditional residential architecture.

The structure floats above the landscape, minimizing its footprint while maximizing panoramic views of the Pacific coastline. Utilizing a cantilevered steel frame and floor-to-ceiling thermal glass curtains, the residence dissolves the barrier between the inhabitant and nature.

The material palette—polished concrete, warm walnut, and matte black steel—provides a grounding contrast to the ethereal transparency of the envelope. Every room is designed to capture specific light throughout the day, turning the home into a living sundial.

Design Philosophy
"Minimalism isn't about absence; it's about the perfect amount of something. We strip away the unnecessary to reveal the essential beauty of structure and light."
